## How about a little backstory?

I started running just two years ago in the summer of 2024. While I'm still relatively new to the sport compared to most others, that doesn't mean I don't push my limits. I am always after setting new personal bests and seeing what I am capable of. 

I am not incredibly fast and not really above average yet. My current personal bests are 5:39 in the 1600m and 20:15 in the 5000, as my main events.
